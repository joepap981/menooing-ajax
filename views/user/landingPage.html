Services

Eaters & Chefs is a coworking restaurant space that provides the necessities to operate a restaurant at an affordable price. New restauranteurs can rent space, equipment, and managerial assistance that we provide and minimize the entry cost, risk and workload that it takes to start a restaurant.

Fully Furbished

The highest of quality equipment and everything else you need to run a restaurant is provided.

Backend Assistance

Health inspections, cleanup, marketing – we’ll do the heavy lifting, you do what you do best: make food.

Omnichannel: Total Access

Consumers will have access to their meals in all channels: dine in, take out, online ordering and delivery.

Who can make the best of our service?

Chefs

Chefs who can have the culinary skills but need help on running a restaurant business.

Food Entrepreneurs

Someone who wants to venture into the restaurant industry with low risk and low capital investment.

Caterers

Those who need a temporary licensed preparation space for food preparation.

About

The journey to where we are today.

  • 2015

    Menooing 1.0

    Mennooing starts as a third party delivery consultation company.

  • 2017

    Menooing 2.0 - 5.0

    Menooing starts testing with the concept of a shared kitchen restaurant, creating and operating 22 "virtual restaurants".

  • February 2018

    Eaters8

    The virtual restaurants get their own brick and mortar restaurant, Eaters8: a foodhall concept where 8 different brands share a kitchen.

  • 2018

    Mini Eaters & Chefs Launching

    The kitchen space rental service kicks off within Eaters8, providing everything a chef needs to run a restaurant.
